Steps followed to download and extract GHCN daily data for PathFinder.
Contact: Kjetil Aas kjetil.aas@cicero.oslo.no

Download (accessed 17.06.2025):
wget https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/data/global-historical-climatology-network-daily/archive/daily-summaries-latest.tar.gz

Extract:
tar -xvzf daily-summaries-latest.tar.gz
